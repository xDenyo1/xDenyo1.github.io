6. Website Comparison

Selected Websites:

Amazon (https://www.amazon.com) and Alibaba (https://www.alibaba.com)

Repetition:
Both websites use repetition by maintaining consistent layouts, colors, and navigation menus across all pages. For example, product listings follow a similar structure throughout the site.

Contrast:
Amazon uses strong contrast with orange and black to highlight important elements like buttons and prices. Alibaba also uses contrast to emphasize product categories and call-to-action buttons.

Proximity:
Both sites group related items together. Product details, images, and prices are placed close to each other, making it easier for users to understand the content quickly.

Alignment:
Both websites use clean alignment. Content is arranged in grids, ensuring that everything looks organized and visually appealing.
