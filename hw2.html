Homework #2 - Website Analysis

a) What is the URL of the site?

The URL of the website is: https://xdenyo1.github.io

b) What is the purpose of the site?

The purpose of this website is to present personal information and homework assignments. It is designed as a personal learning portfolio for web development.

c) Who is the intended audience?

The intended audience includes the course instructor, classmates, and anyone interested in web development.

d) Do you think that the site reaches its intended audience? Why or why not?

Yes, the website reaches its intended audience because it provides clear navigation and direct access to homework pages. It allows the instructor to easily review assignments.

e) Is the site useful to you? Why or why not?

Yes, the site is useful because it helps organize and present all assignments in one place. It also shows my progress in learning web development.

f) Does this site appeal to you? Why or why not?

The site is simple and easy to use. Navigation is clear and straightforward. However, it has a basic design and could be improved with better colors, images, and styling.

g) Would you encourage others to visit this site? Why or why not?

Yes, I would encourage others to visit this site because it is a good example of a beginner-level personal website.

h) How could this site be improved?

The site could be improved by adding CSS for better design, including images, creating a responsive layout, and improving overall visual appearance.
